By default Ubuntu-22.04 installs ansible 2.10.8, but fortiflex ansible collection ver2+ needs ansible 2.15+

Installing latest Ansible on Ubuntu-22.04, and installing fortinet.fortiflexvm v2 collection:

apt-add-repository ppa:ansible/ansible
apt update
apt install ansible
